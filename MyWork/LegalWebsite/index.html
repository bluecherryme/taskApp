The Riverina Law Firm

We care about you, your family, your business and our communities.

Glowreys-The Riverina Law Firm was established by Ed and Jane Glowrey from the previous legal practice conducted by Jon Semple, initially on his own, and later with Peter Long. After its establishment it incorporated the legal practice of Schneider and Sibree. Both Jon Semple’s, and Schneider and Sibree’s, practices were , in turn, founded on the practice of Norman Sheehan.

With the next generation of professionals, in the form of Solicitor John Glowrey, and Licensed Conveyancer Johanna Staedel, involved, the firm is looking to the future, with a determination to build on its existing base, and continue to provide comprehensive legal services to the region.
